CONTINUOUSLY EVOLVING.

My journey is the source of my strength!

How yoga
affected me.

Yoga has been a transformative part of my life, offering both physical and mental benefits that have helped me grow in ways I never expected. I started practicing yoga several years ago, initially as a way to relieve stress and improve my flexibility. Over time, I came to appreciate its deeper aspects, such as mindfulness, meditation, and self-awareness. My practice has evolved with me. Whether it's the calming effect of restorative poses after a long day or the challenge of an intense flow sequence, yoga has taught me the importance of balance, patience, and persistence. I've studied various styles, from Hatha and Vinyasa to Yin, and have learned something valuable from each.

My Yoga Journey and IYA Certification

My yoga practice has been deeply shaped by the teachings of Andiappan Yoga and the guidance I received through the IYA International Yoga Academy. I am proud to hold an official IYA Certification in Yoga, a recognition that has not only deepened my practice but also refined my understanding of yoga’s transformative power.

The IYA International Yoga Academy is known for its comprehensive, high-quality training programs, and receiving my certification from them was a pivotal moment in my journey. The curriculum provided a holistic approach to yoga, combining physical postures, breath control, and meditation techniques with a deeper understanding of yoga philosophy and its therapeutic benefits.

Shivam Yoga
Studio

My journey has now led me to Shivam Yoga Studio which is internationally recognized Registered Yoga School (RYS 500), which represents a more advanced and holistic phase of my practice. Unlike anything I’ve experienced before, Shivam Yoga integrates dynamic postures with powerful breathing techniques, mantra chanting, and meditative awareness that go beyond the physical. It challenges not just the body, but also the inner self, pushing me to explore yoga as a lifestyle rather than a routine. Each session feels like a step closer to clarity, discipline, and inner peace. I now understand how yoga can be a path to transformation at every level—physically, mentally, emotionally, and spiritually. Shivam Yoga has helped me discover deeper layers of strength and stillness I never knew existed within me.
